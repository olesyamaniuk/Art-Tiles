How to Play

  • Pick a Puzzle: Choose from a wide variety of artistic themes, from classic to modern.

  • Use Hints: Stuck on a tricky section? Use hints to reveal the correct placement of tiles.

  • Arrange the Pieces: Drag and drop puzzle tiles into the correct positions to complete the artwork.

  • Complete Challenges: Solve daily and weekly challenges to earn rewards and unlock exclusive puzzles.

  • Customize Your Experience: Adjust difficulty levels, background colors, and more to suit your preferences.
